The ‘Like’ as a concept is largely worthless. A ‘Like’ has no value, no cost for doling out. ‘Liking’ your content costs me nothing, so what’s to stop me from ‘Liking’ everything I see without considering how it affects the popularity of that content?

Like it or not (pun not initially intended), the ways in which many sites rank and suggest content is now often based heavily on ‘Likes’.
